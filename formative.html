Student Mental Health


My data is from this dataset

Many students struggle with their mental health whilst studying

This webpage aims to provide some useful information and links to help you understand and combat poor mental health and additionally to allow you to check-in with yourself to evaluate how you're doing.

Some interesting facts about Student Mental Health

35% of students answered that they have depression, which is a very high figure compared to the World Health Organization's statement that "5% of adults suffer from depression" globally.

Additionally, 34% of students responded that they also suffer from anxiety. Another staggering figure when compared to the World Health Organisation's estimation that 3.6% of the global population suffer with anxiety disorders.
This data is from 2015 as it is the most recent information released by the WHO, so following events such as Covid-19 - these figures will likely have increased a bit but it's highly unlikely they have reached the levels of the students on this dataset.

The data also collected that 33% of the students experience panic attacks. Compared to the estimation that roughly 4% of US adults have panic attacks at some point in their lives, this is yet another large difference who's relevance is often overlooked.
